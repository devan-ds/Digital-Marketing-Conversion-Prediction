# Digital-Marketing-Conversion-Prediction

In today's digital world, businesses spend huge amounts on marketing campaigns, but not everyone who clicks on an ad actually makes a purchase. This project aims to solve that problem using machine learning to predict which customers are most likely to convert. By analyzing demographics, customer engagement, and marketing channels, we uncover key insights—like how referral marketing and PPC campaigns drive the highest conversions. After cleaning and preparing the data, we tested various models, with Random Forest emerging as the best performer, achieving 97% accuracy after fine-tuning. With this model, businesses can make smarter marketing decisions, focus on high-potential customers, and ultimately maximize their ROI, ensuring their advertising budget is spent wisely.
